ACCOMODATION

The Bridal Party and their families will be staying at the Secura Lifestyle holiday park and they would love for you to join them. There are various 2 bedroom cabins and campsites available to book. We are staying 4 nights from Wednesday 19th Feb until Sunday 23rd Feb.

All cabins have been reserved for the wedding party. To book a cabin, please call the holiday park directly on 02 6555 5511 to enquire on availability. Let them know it's for the Tyson and Jess wedding to receive a 15% discount (do not try to book online as it shows fully booked). If the holiday park is unable to find suitable accomdation, contact Jess and she can help!

If Secura Lifestyle cabins or campsite options do not accommodate your needs, there are plenty of other accommodation nearby.

TRANSPORTATION

Forster is a 3.5 hour drive north of Sydney airport and 1.5 / 2 hour drive north of Newcastle airport.We recommend from Sydney & Newcastle hiring a car to get to the venue. You can also fly into Port Macquarie but the flights and car hire are twice the price and is 1 hour from the venue.
